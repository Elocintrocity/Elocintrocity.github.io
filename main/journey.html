About Journey(current) Lessons

It wasn't until February of this year (2018) that my interest was piqued. My Computer Science degree led me to be in multiple electives my junior year and it wasn't until after taking Computer Security I (CS461) and halfway into Security Lab (CS460) that I realized I'm really enjoy the material way more than anything else I learned so far in my courses. This pushed me into seeing what else is out there in this field. Very soon after, I got bombarded with the myriad of sections that Cyber Security encompasses and even to this day I still don't know exactly what I want to do. Over the months and especially after finals this year, I an starting to make a more concrete decision on where I want to start in this field.
One thing's for sure: I need to start NOW.
It's one thing to say you're interested in this field, and it's another thing entirely to show that you are. Hence my summer plans:

  • Get my CompTIA Security+ Certification
  • Review and monitor the job boards of the companies I'm interested in and take notes on their preferred experiences
  • Connect with a possible mentor, or network with people who can help guide me to what entry level positions want to see
  • ??? (TBD)
  • Profit (?) Yet to be decided!

    • It's a long road, but at the same time it's a dangerously short road. Time is ticking, my site is a bombardment of thoughts and hopes, but I know I can accomplish all of this and more even with my summer research I have lined up. One day I'll look back at this list and add more, but for now, this is what I need to do.
